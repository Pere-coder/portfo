03 : About me

Godspower Pere Toikumo

Godspower Pere Toikumo is a Biologist, graphic designer, and python developer focusing on web development using Django, with experience in Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, python, and also in teaching High school biology. Godspower is fueled by his passion for understanding how graphics can be used to create a powerful impression and communicate a design to people, Programming and how it can be used to solve real problems, biology and how It can be applied to creating a better environment for living organisms. He considers himself a ‘forever student', eager to both solve problems with his skills and also stay in tune by constantly updating his skill set through continued study and course work.

Godspower’s hunger for knowledge and determination to turn information into action has contributed to his success in being able to successfully managing projects ranging from graphic design to web development using python. He is passionate about rendering services and helping out in any way he can, willing to understand and adapt to solve an existing problem.

Godspower is willing to face new challenges and is an adept problem solver, he is a great team worker but can also manage projects alone, with little or no supervision. Godpower is currently working as a freelance graphic designer and web developer and is always interested in a challenge.
